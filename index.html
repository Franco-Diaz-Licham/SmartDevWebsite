About Me

Hi, I'm Franco Diaz, a freelance Software Engineer based in Western Sydney, Australia. SmartDev was established as a passion project in my after-work hours after a simple realisation: many businesses were struggling to transition from outdated, time-consuming, paper-based processes to efficient, fully online systems. After working alongside companies of different sizes and industries, I saw that companies don't just want an impersonal approach, they want personal, custom solutions with a person who seeks to understand and provide detailed advice and guidance.

I specialise in creating custom software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, I'm passionate about designing solutions that fit your business and help you succeed. I'm committed to making technology approachable and empowering my clients to fully embrace the tools that will help them thrive.
